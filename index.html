        <h2 id="basecamp">Cofounder of Basecamp & HEY</h2>

        <p>

            <a href="https://basecamp.com">Basecamp</a>

             is the one-place-not-all-over-the-place way to organize your projects and your company. Launched in 

            <a href="https://signalvnoise.com/archives/000542.php">2004</a>

             as one of the first software-as-a-service applications, and used by millions of people since. It's the original Rails application.

        </p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://hey.com">HEY</a>

             is a fresh approach to turning email into something you want to use, not something you’re forced to deal with. A complete alternative to Gmail or Outlook for individuals and companies who want a better relationship with email. Tens of thousands of customers have embraced it since we launched in 2020.

        </p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://37signals.com">37signals</a>

             is the company behind both Basecamp and HEY. I own that business together with Jason Fried.

        </p>


        <h2 id="rework">Best-selling author</h2>

        <p>I've written four books together with Jason Fried. All on the lessons we've extracted from running 37signals in an unconventional way over the last twenty-plus years.</p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://basecamp.com/books/rework/">REWORK</a>

             is a New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and Sunday Times best seller with more than half a million copies sold. It's a book about starting and running businesses a better way.

        </p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://basecamp.com/books/calm/">It Doesn't Have To Be Crazy At Work</a>

             is about how to run a calm company by avoiding the lure of excessive growth and making your time count for more.

        </p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://basecamp.com/books/remote/">REMOTE: Office Not Required</a>

             encapsulated all we'd learned  working remotely for over a decade. Ahead of its time, but suddenly extremely relevant after 2020.

        </p>


        <p>

            <a href="https://basecamp.com/books/getting-real/">Getting Real</a>

             shared everything we learned building Basecamp and a software business back in the early days. Still on point.

        </p>


        <h2 id="driver">Le Mans & WEC class-winning racing driver</h2>

        <p>

            I raced in the FIA 

            <a href="https://www.fiawec.com">World Endurance Championship</a>

             for seven years. In 2014, I won my class with Aston Martin in 

            <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mWR0B8LvA3g">the 82nd running of the 24 hours of Le Mans</a>

             – the world's biggest endurance race. I also won 

            <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nkYQPBYWJQo">the WEC championship</a>

             in the GTE-Am category that year. But mostly, I 

            <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iNQl0x6WS3M">raced in LMP2 prototypes</a>

            . I've finished on the podium at Le Mans four times in nine appearances. In 2023, 

            <a href="https://sportscar365.com/lemans/wec/jota-signs-fittipaldi-heinemeier-hansson-to-lmp2-program/">I'm back in the FIA WEC</a>

            , driving another LMP2 for JOTA Sport.

        </p>


        <h2 id="antitrust">Antitrust advocate</h2>

        <p>

            Big tech monopolies are in urgent need of being brought back under control of democracies. I've been testifying in favor of antitrust action before the 

            <a href="https://m.signalvnoise.com/testimony-before-the-house-antitrust-subcommittee/">House Antitrust Subcommittee</a>

            , the 

            <a href="https://m.signalvnoise.com/on-apples-monopoly-power-to-destroy-hey/">democratic side of the House Antitrust Subcommittee</a>

            , the 

            <a href="https://m.signalvnoise.com/testimony-before-the-north-dakota-senate-industry-business-and-labor-committee/">North Dakota Senate</a>

            , and the 

            <a href="https://world.hey.com/dhh/testimony-before-the-arizona-house-of-representatives-e48f3e89">Arizona House of Representatives</a>

            . As well as spoken at length with US and EU regulators about the problem. We had 

            <a href="https://www.hey.com/apple/">a big monopoly-power battle with Apple</a>

             when we launched HEY.

        </p>
